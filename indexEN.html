Our Services

Transporation Service

We provide a transportation service for dangerous products, implementing both driver supervision and monitoring for the company.

Real time monitoring

We offer a real-time monitoring system that can detect possible leaks of combustible gases (NGV) during the transportation of these.

Usage of IoT Sensors

The vehicles will have sensors that will monitor the material load and will be notified if there is a leak. They will be sensors for temperature, pressure, movement, etc.

Safe Transport

Together we can transport dangerous materials and products without no kind of danger. Let us help you and take care of you in all your crossing.

Who are we?

SecureOn was born from the need for a company dedicated to managing safety in the transportation of dangerous products. Our mission is to help transporters feel safer and more protected in their jobs, from less dangerous materials to those that are highly risky and can harm people's health.

Join and be part of this family, where by helping each other we guarantee peaceful and worry-free transportation.
